Situated directly on the borders of the Okertalsperre.


A camping site at an enchanting location in the forests, you can swim, surf, sail, row, fish or just simply enjoy the day.

We still have some beautiful annual pitches available.

You can ask for more information by telephone or e-mail. But of course you're also very welcome to visit our camping site for a view.

We're always there for you.
